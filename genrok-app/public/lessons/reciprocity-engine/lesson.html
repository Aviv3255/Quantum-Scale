The Reciprocity Engine

Humans are wired with a simple rule:

When someone gives you something, you feel compelled to give back.

It's not manipulation. It's biology.

We HATE the feeling of being indebted. It creates psychological tension. Discomfort.

The only way to release that tension? Reciprocate.

Those candy shop customers felt they "owed" the store something. Even though the sample was genuinely free, with no expectation.

So they bought. Not because they wanted candy. Because they wanted to discharge the social debt.
