How to Implement This Strategy

Step-by-step guide to setting up affiliate partnerships

  • Identify Customer Needs

    Survey your customers or analyze support tickets to find common questions or needs you don't fulfill.

  • Research Complementary Brands

    Find high-quality brands that serve those needs without competing with your core products.

  • Reach Out to Potential Partners

    Present the win-win opportunity: you bring them customers, they pay you commission.

  • Negotiate Commission Structure

    Typical rates: 10-30% for services, 5-15% for physical products. Negotiate based on order value.

  • Set Up Tracking & Links

    Use affiliate platforms (ShareASale, Impact, Refersion) or custom tracking codes to ensure proper attribution.

  • Integrate into Customer Journey

    Add recommendations to post-purchase emails, thank you pages, newsletters, or inside product packaging.

  • Test & Optimize

    Monitor click-through and conversion rates. A/B test messaging, placement, and timing.

  • Scale What Works

    Once you find winning partnerships, expand: add more touchpoints, negotiate better rates, find similar partners.
