How To Apply This To Your Business

For Product Creators:

  • Identify the "complete solution" your customer wants
  • Deliver 70-80% in the core offer (so it works and provides value)
  • Make the missing 20-30% a natural next step (not forced)
  • Example: Sell the course, upsell the community + templates

For Ecosystem Builders:

  • Design products that share data or functionality
  • Make cross-product features genuinely better (not fake)
  • Reward customers who use multiple products

Critical Rule: The core product must deliver value on its own. If it feels broken without the upsell, you've crossed into manipulation.
