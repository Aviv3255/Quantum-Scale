Calculate Your True CAC

Follow these steps to uncover your real numbers

  • List ALL your acquisition channels
    Paid ads, organic, partnerships, cold outreach, content, etc.
  • Track costs per channel for 30 days
    Ad spend, payroll, software, commissions, your time (use replacement cost)
  • Count new customers per channel
    Use attribution properly‚Äîfirst touch, last touch, or multi-touch based on your model
  • Calculate: Total Costs √∑ New Customers
    Do this separately for EACH channel, not as a blended average
  • Compare to your LTV
    Healthy ratio: LTV should be 3x+ your CAC. Below 2x? You're in trouble.
