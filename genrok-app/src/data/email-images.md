## Usage Guidelines

### Email Template Recommendations

| Email Type | Recommended Categories |
|------------|----------------------|
| Welcome Series | Beauty, Women's Fashion, Lifestyle |
| Abandoned Cart | Flat Lay, Product shots, Accessories |
| Post-Purchase | Lifestyle, Nature, Brand storytelling |
| Win-Back | Beauty portraits, Fashion editorial |
| Flash Sale | Flat lay, Product details, Accessories |
| VIP/Loyalty | Premium fashion, Jewelry, Luxury lifestyle |

### Image Sizing for Emails
- **Full-width hero**: 600px wide (will scale)
- **Two-column**: 280px each
- **Thumbnail**: 150px × 150px
- Always use `width` attribute, not CSS, for email compatibility
